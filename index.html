ABOUT ME

WHO I AM

I am a Career Practitioner and an author who lives in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Work for the Centre for Newcomers. I am a published author of four works and many unpublished manuscripts waiting to go to the press. Among my most important work is the book Destination Canada: Tools for Success for New Canadians.

Being a Certified Career Development Professional (CCDP) and a member of the Career Development Association of Alberta, I have over 20 years of experience in career counselling, specialising in counselling new Canadians from Western and Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and the Caribbean. This experience and passion to help people led me to write my book, Destination Canada - Tools for Success for New Canadians.
